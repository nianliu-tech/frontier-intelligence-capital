# Brand Notes

## Brand Positioning

**Frontier Intelligence Capital** should feel:

- AI-native, not AI-hype
- professional, but not old Wall Street
- founder-led
- long-term oriented
- technology-literate
- calm and thesis-driven

## Keywords

- Intelligence
- Frontier
- Compute
- Productivity
- Ownership
- Capital allocation
- Public markets
- Long-term compounding
- Changing economics of work

## Avoid

- overly crypto-like language
- “get rich with AI” tone
- aggressive day-trading language
- vague “AI revolution” clichés without thesis
- too much fear-based “workers replaced by AI” framing

## Preferred Tone

Use:

> AI is changing the economics of work.

Instead of:

> AI is replacing workers.

Use:

> ownership of productive assets

Instead of:

> escaping employment

Use:

> public market exposure to AI-driven productivity growth

Instead of:

> betting on AI stocks
