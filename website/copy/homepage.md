# Frontier Intelligence Capital

## Investing in the Age of Intelligence

An AI-native public markets investment company focused on technology, productivity, and the changing economics of work.

AI is reshaping the relationship between labor and capital. We focus on owning the platforms, infrastructure, and assets positioned to compound from this shift.

## Focus Areas

- AI Infrastructure
- Compute & Semiconductors
- Cloud Platforms
- Software Productivity
- Nasdaq / Public Market Exposure

## Core Thesis

The most durable way to participate in the upside of intelligence is not only to use AI, but to own the assets, platforms, and companies that compound from it.
