01 — Core Belief
Investment lens Compute · Platforms · Public Ownership · Risk Control

AI is not only a software cycle. It is a structural shift in how economic value is created, captured, and compounded.

As intelligence becomes increasingly abundant, the relationship between labor, capital, compute, platforms, and ownership is being reshaped.

The deeper question is not which AI tools to use, but who captures the economic surplus AI creates.

Frontier Intelligence Capital focuses on that ownership layer through liquid public markets, with emphasis on technology platforms, AI infrastructure, software productivity, and disciplined exposure management.

The most durable way to participate in the upside of intelligence is not only to use AI, but to own the productive assets that compound from it.

03 — Framework
Portfolio construction

Core exposure first. Leverage second. Fragility always measured.

The framework is designed to participate in long-term technology compounding while keeping duration, liquidity, concentration, and downside structure visible.

Core Own the index layer QQQ / Nasdaq-100 as the primary exposure to public technology compounding.
Leverage Use structure, not speculation LEAPS and QLD only when duration, liquidity, and downside are controlled.
Risk Migrate gains toward durability Profits move from fragile instruments toward ownership layers or reserves.
