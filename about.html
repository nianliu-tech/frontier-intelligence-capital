About

Frontier Intelligence Capital is an AI-native public markets investment company.

The company focuses on long-term exposure to technology, productivity, and the changing economics of work in the age of artificial intelligence.

Its research framework centers on public market assets that may compound from AI infrastructure, platform distribution, software productivity, and the broader shift from labor-intensive knowledge work toward scalable capital and compute.
