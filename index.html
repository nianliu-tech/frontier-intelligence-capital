Investing in the Age of Intelligence.

Frontier Intelligence Capital is an AI-native public markets research vehicle focused on technology, productivity, and the changing economics of work — built around long-term ownership of the assets that compound from artificial intelligence.

Read the Manifesto Investment Thesis 2026
01 — Core Belief

AI is not only a software cycle. It is a structural shift in how economic value is created, captured, and compounded.

As intelligence becomes increasingly abundant, the relationship between labor, capital, compute, platforms, and ownership is being reshaped. The deeper question is not which AI tools to use, but who captures the economic surplus AI creates.

The most durable way to participate in the upside of intelligence is not only to use AI, but to own the productive assets that compound from it.
02 — Focus

II.

III.

Software Productivity

IV.

Public Market Exposure

Liquid access to long-term technology compounding through disciplined allocation.

03 — Philosophy
  • Ownership over labor dependency. As AI changes the labor market, ownership of productive assets becomes increasingly important.
  • Long-term exposure over short-term speculation. We are not chasing daily narratives. We focus on durable technology trends.
  • Compounding with risk control. Leverage can accelerate returns, but only if duration, drawdown, and concentration are managed.
  • Public markets as access layer. Many of the most important AI beneficiaries are already public.
  • Clarity over complexity. A good strategy should be explainable, repeatable, and resilient under stress.
