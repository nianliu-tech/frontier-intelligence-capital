## About Section — Full

After years in technology, I became increasingly convinced that AI will reshape not only software, but also the economics of work itself.

Frontier Intelligence Capital is my vehicle for investing in that structural shift. The focus is public market exposure to technology platforms, AI infrastructure, software productivity, and long-term compounding assets.

Rather than treating AI as a short-term theme, I view it as a multi-year reallocation of economic power toward compute, platforms, data, ownership, and capital allocation.

My work focuses on disciplined exposure to this transition through public markets, with an emphasis on risk control, duration management, and long-term technology compounding.

## Company Description

Frontier Intelligence Capital is an AI-native public markets investment company focused on technology, productivity, and the changing economics of work.

We invest around the belief that AI is reshaping the relationship between labor and capital, and that long-term ownership of productive technology assets is one of the clearest ways to participate in this transition.
